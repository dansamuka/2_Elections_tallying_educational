Methodology & limitations
Sentiment is estimated with VADER (English) plus a small hand-built Kiswahili/Sheng lexicon with negation handling; content the models can't confidently read is labelled unscored rather than forced into a score. Candidate-topic cells with fewer than five independent items are suppressed. Alerts require corroboration across multiple independent sources before they're shown — a single post, however repeated, never triggers one on its own. No indicator on this page predicts a winner or converts sentiment into vote share. Official results come from IEBC. Nothing on this page reproduces individual posts, usernames, or links to individual accounts — trending terms and hashtags are aggregate counts against a pre-configured vocabulary, and news items link only to already-public articles.
